    Mint Importance of Mint.

    Mint is a common ingredient in oral care products like toothpaste, mouthwash, and chewing gum. Its natural antibacterial properties help freshen breath, kill harmful bacteria in the mouth, and provide a cooling sensation. Mint's association with oral hygiene and cleanliness has contributed to its widespread use in dental products.

    Neem Importance of Neem

    Neem (Azadirachta indica) is a tree native to the Indian subcontinent and has been used for centuries in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for its medicinal properties. Every part of the neem tree—leaves, bark, seeds, and oil—holds potential benefits.Neem leaves are traditionally used to promote digestive health. They might help alleviate digestive issues like indigestion and bloating.
